Guard Well Identity Theft Protection 2024 Review

By ASR Staff - Consumer Expert
ASR Rating
See Best Price

Expert Review

Guardwell ID offers identity theft solutions to both consumers and businesses alike, and they provide a rather practical vision of the real truth behind the identity theft risk that we all face every day. The company believes in offering the best resolution process available, so that all members can get their finances and reputation back to normal as quickly as possible.

What We Like

We appreciate anything that sounds honest, because theres far too much noise surrounding the messages of identity theft companies. While everyone else is boasting about the billions of data points theyre constantly checking, Guardwell is posting statistics about how monitoring is only about 20% effective. They call to attention that financial identity theft is not the only thing you need to be worried about, and that your children are more likely to be targets than you think. They have an A+ rating with the BBB too.

To their credit, Guardwell offers resolution for the entire family even at the lowest level of the plans. They give you access to a designated fraud specialist and proactive means for everyone to start safeguarding their identity before anything happens. Youll be able to reach a full team of fraud specialists at any time of day, so you can rest easy knowing you have someone to call no matter where you are.

What We Dont Like

As much as we love the idea that theyll be there for their customers after something happens, they really dont seem to offer as much support as other companies when it comes to the preventative side of it. Yes, monitoring has its flaws, but it can often be those alerts that stop situations from getting out of control. They certainly offer these services of course, but consumer reviews seem to confirm that theyre much more interested in starting their services once your security has actually been breached.

Guardwell also doesnt offer insurance or any type of power of attorney. While exact standards in identity theft protection are hard to find, these are often two components that seem to really bail people out when they need full restoration of their identity.

The Bottom Line

The company may well be an up-and-comer in the identity theft world, but you may want to wait until they get more experience before you sign up with them. Time will teach them whats working and what isnt, and if they can get their philosophies straight then they may be a truly solid choice. As of right now though, you may want to go with another carrier.

How Guardwell ID Protection Works

As mentioned, Guardwell puts the bulk of its emphasis on the resolution part of the process. All of their plans offer the initial three benefits in this list.

  • 24/7 Access to Fraud Specialists: Receive full assistance should something happen to you, even when youre out of town.
  • Personal Assigned Specialist: Work with one person for your case, so you wont have to worry about explaining yourself again and again.
  • Complete Resolution: Guardwell promises to detect problems early, stop thieves in their tracks and start performing the necessary steps to reclaim your identity.
  • Credit Reports and Monitoring: Get your scores so you know more about your financial situation, and receive alerts so youre aware of whats happening in the world with your information. You also have access to a score tracker, so you can see which financial decisions are paying off and which arent.
  • Personal Monitoring: Guardwell goes beyond just the financial aspect of identity theft, and also provides monitoring on your social security number and any change of address notices filled out in your name. Also includes the monitoring of check-cashing companies and other loan businesses as well as court and virtual criminal records.
  • Sex Offender Registry: Guardwell continuously monitors your neighborhood and gives notice if theres a registered sex offender near you.
