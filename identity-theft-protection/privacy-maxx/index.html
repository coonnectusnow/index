PrivacyMaxx uses advanced pattern detection across a variety of platforms to determine when and where a thief may have obtained a customers data before they have a chance to cause trouble. They look for the most common indicators of a breach, such as address discrepancies, and then send an alert immediately. PrivacyMaxx offers total family protection in both of their plans.

The concept of their Early Warning Detection search algorithm shows the proactive nature of the company, and the want to stop criminals before they even have a chance to make it past the first step of their plan. Considering they can pay out up to $1,000,000 depending on your plan, they have every incentive to make the system work for everyones advantage. What We Dont Like

This is not a BBB accredited company, and there arent a lot of consumer reviews online. The main complaints seem associated with customer service or the lack thereof, especially in cancelling the service after the 30 day trial. Also, you need to sign up for 6 months if you choose the basic plan. They do not offer Antivirus software in case theres an attack on your personal computer which is a growing concern from overseas hackers. In terms of credit protection, according to their website, they will only walk you through how to get your free credit report, as opposed to monitoring and sending you the report instead. Theres also virtually no outside information about just how well their Early Warning Detection actually works. The fact that they do have PrivacyMaxx Labs, a division of their company, continuously working on finding the best innovations in monitoring does suggest again that their approach is proactive. Considering how creative hackers can be and the fact that they change their methods as fast as the software can update, it shows that their focus is on the right track. You may see some upgrades to the plans in the coming years as they work out which services they want to offer. Theyve also responded to online customer complaints and taken proper action.

The Bottom Line

This all comes down to whether or not you trust their particular monitoring systems, so you may want to wait to see if theres more concrete evidence in the future. It does not look like there are enough people using this service to determine one way or the other. However, they are performing a service you likely wouldnt be able to do on your own, and again, it does seem as though theyre looking ahead in terms of where identity theft might be headed. There is a lot of data out there, and if it continuously sifts through it all the way Privacy Maxx claims, then you do stand a better chance at stopping the problem before it starts.

How PrivacyMaxx Works

There are several main points stressed for their services, alongside a host of other protection methods for everyone in the family.

  • Warning System: PrivacyMaxx determines which data points to scan based on patterns from real identity theft cases which is meant to target the hackers quickly. The upgraded plan offers an even more aggressive searching algorithm, constantly checking and rechecking credit and utility information in addition to the normal sources.
  • Recovery: Both plans insure both you and and your family against identity theft. If you are a victim, they offer impressive recovery services including the option to either let them assist you or just to turn over the hassle to them. General protection provides about $25,000 against the costs associated with identity theft, including lost wages that you may have taking care of the many practical matters associated with identity theft.
  • Health: PrivacyMaxx will send you the health reports that your insurance has, so you can verify the information. Find out more about how they assess your, your spouses and your childrens risk for various plans.
  • Family: Offers protection for children against internet predators and provides you with the most up-to-date best practices for protecting your kids online. The perils of internet use shift from year to year, and having the right controls in place is just one more step you can take to keep your children safe. Also provides a lost child recovery kit if the worst does occur.
  • Criminal Records: PrivacyMaxx Protector plans also include criminal monitoring, so if a thief is committing crimes in your or one of your family members name, youre alerted through their reports.
  • Lost Wallet Service: Store the information in your physical wallet online, so youre instantly ready if its lost or stolen.
