How Much Does LifeLock Cost?

When it comes to protecting your identity, LifeLock consistently ranks among the top players in the game. They offer a range of plans that suit different protection needs and budgets.

Whether you're someone who just wants the basics or need advanced identity monitoring and fraud alerts, LifeLock has something for everyone.

But how much does it cost? And more importantly, is it worth it? Let's dive in and break down the pricing and benefits of each plan.

The price you'll pay for LifeLock largely depends on the plan you choose. LifeLock offers three main tiers: Standard, Advantage, and Ultimate Plus. Here's what you can expect from each:

LifeLock Standard Plan

  • Starts at $9.99/month for the first year
  • After the first year, pricing increases, depending on promotions and renewals
  • Offers up to $25,000 in stolen funds reimbursement
  • Monitors your Social Security number and the dark web for signs of identity theft

The Standard Plan is a solid choice if you're just getting your feet wet with identity protection. It offers basic coverage that includes Social Security number alerts and dark web monitoring. You'll also be reimbursed up to $25,000 if your funds are stolen.

What Benefits Do All LifeLock Plans Share?

No matter which plan you go with, LifeLock comes with some core benefits that all users can count on. These features help protect you from a wide variety of identity theft risks, providing peace of mind whether you're using the basic or premium service.

  • Stolen Funds Reimbursement

    Each LifeLock plan includes reimbursement if funds are stolen due to identity theft. Depending on your plan, this ranges from $25,000 to $1 million. This protection ensures that if someone gains access to your accounts and drains your funds, you'll get reimbursed up to the coverage limit.

  • Dark Web Monitoring

    LifeLock monitors the dark web for any signs of your personal information being traded or sold. This includes checking for things like your Social Security number, email addresses, credit card numbers, and other personal details that can be used in identity theft schemes.

  • Credit Monitoring and Alerts

    All plans monitor at least one credit bureau for changes that could indicate identity theft. With the Ultimate Plus plan, you'll get alerts across all three bureaus—Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion. Any suspicious activity such as new account openings or large credit inquiries will trigger an alert, giving you the chance to react quickly.

  • Bank and Credit Card Monitoring

    LifeLock will send you alerts if there are any signs of unauthorized access to your bank accounts or credit cards. This could be unusual activity, such as a large transaction that doesn't match your typical spending behavior, or an attempt to take over your bank account.

  • Identity Restoration

    If your identity is compromised, you won't be left to sort through the mess on your own. Each LifeLock plan includes identity restoration support, where a dedicated specialist will work with you to recover your identity and resolve any fraudulent issues.

  • Lost Wallet Protection

    Losing your wallet can be a nightmare. LifeLock helps mitigate the damage by assisting in canceling and replacing credit cards, identification, and other documents. This ensures that any lost information won't fall into the wrong hands.

  • Data Breach Alerts

    With data breaches becoming all too common, LifeLock will notify you if any of the companies you do business with suffer a breach that could expose your personal information. This allows you to take proactive steps, like changing passwords or freezing your credit, before hackers can use your data.

  • Legal Assistance

    For those on the Ultimate Plus plan, LifeLock offers up to $1 million in legal assistance and fees. This is a major plus if identity theft escalates to the point where legal help is needed to clear your name.

Why LifeLock Stands Out

LifeLock is a strong option in a sea of identity protection services, but what really sets it apart is the comprehensive coverage and the identity restoration support that comes with every plan. Whether you're just looking for basic monitoring or need the whole suite of protective services, LifeLock offers peace of mind in a world where identity theft is constantly evolving.

Sure, you might pay more than with some other services, but LifeLock's advanced monitoring and real-time alerts are often worth the extra cost, especially when you factor in things like credit bureau monitoring, bank alerts, and stolen funds reimbursement. Plus, their restoration specialists are there to guide you through the worst-case scenarios, which can be invaluable if your identity is ever stolen.
